Family Perspective

People with schizophrenia have had a more negative effect on their families. In the families perspective, schizophrenia has been the reason for social discrimination, higher anxiety, and negatively affected family lives.

A family's response to having a family member with schizophrenia can include feelings of embarrassment, lack of social support, and having the feeling of this disorder being a burden on them.

  1. Families that have a family member with schizophrenia have reported that they have been victim to social discrimination.
  2. Parents with a child with schizophrenia have reported having higher anxiety and depression.
  3. Those that are married to someone with schizophrenia have reported that their family life has been negatively affected, and friendships lost.
